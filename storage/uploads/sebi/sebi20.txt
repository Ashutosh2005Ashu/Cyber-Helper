SEBI circular MRD/DoP/SE/Cir-03/2009 permits institutional investors to use Direct Market Access (DMA) through SEBI-registered investment managers under specified conditions.
Purpose and Scope
The circular was issued to clarify and extend the Direct Market Access (DMA) facility introduced earlier by SEBI through circular MRD/DoP/SE/Cir-7/2008 dated April 3, 2008. It specifically allows institutional investors to access DMA via investment managers, ensuring proper authorization, documentation, and compliance with SEBI and exchange requirements

Summary
SEBI circular MRD/DoP/SE/Cir-03/2009 primarily enables institutional investors to access DMA via investment managers, establishes documentation and compliance requirements, and ensures auditability and investor protection. It forms a key regulatory framework for DMA operations in India, balancing efficiency in trading with risk management and accountability
